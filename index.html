Welcome to Our School

Jakande Estate Comprehensive Senior College is committed to raising disciplined, knowledgeable, and future-ready students. Our school provides a supportive learning environment where students grow academically, socially, and morally.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that inspires excellence and lifelong learning.

Student Activities

Our students engage in practical, hands-on learning experiences that help them apply classroom knowledge to real-world situations.

Field Observation: Students carrying out practical agricultural research by examining crops and recording data using digital tools.

STEM Workshop: Students learn electronics assembly and circuitry through hands-on experiments.

Collaborative Digital Learning: Students use ICT tools to support teamwork, research, and environmental studies.
